About

This page gives user the ability to schedule when they need to take their prescription medications and warns them of shortages or recalls within the last month. Users will input the number of doses they received, the schedule they need to take them on, and will be able to log days they missed while having the calendar automatically update to reflect the additional doses. Additionally, the page will contain a news tab that will directly link to NYT articles about the healthcare sphere.
